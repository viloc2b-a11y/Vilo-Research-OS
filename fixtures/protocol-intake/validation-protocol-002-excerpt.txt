Protocol Number: VALIDATION_PROTOCOL_002
Protocol Title: Household Transmission Observational Study
Brief Title: VALIDATION_PROTOCOL_002 household surveillance
Sponsor: Public Health Research Collaborative
Phase: 2
Indication: Respiratory virus household transmission
Investigational Product: Not applicable — observational surveillance
Study Design: Prospective household cohort with index patient and household contacts
Enrollment target: 200 index patients
Study Duration: 8 weeks per household

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Index patient with laboratory-confirmed infection within 7 days.
2. At least one household contact willing to enroll.
3. Residence in study catchment area.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Household unable to complete home visit logistics.
2. Index patient requiring immediate hospitalization.

Schedule of Events:
Visit 1: Index screening (window -7 / 0) — site visit index patient
Visit 2: Index enrollment / baseline (window -1 / +3) — site visit index patient
Visit 3: Household contact enrollment Day 5 (window -2 / +5) — site visit household contact
Visit 4: Index home nasal swab Day 7 (window -2 / +2) — home visit index patient
Visit 5: Contact home nasal swab Day 7 (window -2 / +2) — home visit household contact
Visit 6: Week 2 phone check-in Day 14 (window -2 / +2) — phone visit index patient and household contact
Visit 7: Remote symptom follow-up Day 21 (window -3 / +3) — remote visit
Visit 8: Unscheduled sick visit (off-site) — if symptoms worsen
Visit 9: End of study Day 56 (window -7 / +7) — site visit

Study Procedures:
Procedure: Informed Consent (required)
Procedure: Household linkage confirmation (required)
Procedure: Home Nasal Swab (required) — visit: home swab
Procedure: Phone check-in (required)
Procedure: Remote symptom log (required)
Procedure: Unscheduled Sick Assessment (conditional) — sick visit if respiratory symptoms worsen
Procedure: Additional swab if indicated (conditional)
Procedure: AE Assessment (required)
