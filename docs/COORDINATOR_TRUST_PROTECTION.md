# Coordinator Trust Protection

Status: Phase 16G-2 pilot rule

---

## Purpose

Live coordinator observation exists for UX refinement only.

The goal is to find where Vilo OS creates friction, confusion, recovery gaps, unnecessary navigation, or prevention risk so the system can reduce coordinator burden.

---

## Core Principle

Operational friction is a system problem, not a coordinator problem.

The runtime exists to reduce burden, not evaluate humans.

---

## Explicitly Prohibited

Do not create or infer:

- coordinator scoring
- efficiency scoring
- performance ranking
- behavioral surveillance
- comparative coordinator analytics
- sponsor visibility
- CRA tooling
- productivity leaderboards

---

## Allowed Observation

Allowed observation is minimal, humane, and prevention-oriented:

- where the workflow became unclear
- where recovery worked
- where human explanation was required
- where navigation repeated
- where source was reopened multiple times
- what wording caused hesitation
- what should be simplified

---

## How To Use Observation Notes

Use notes to improve:

- readiness language
- operational continuity
- prevention prompts
- recovery paths
- stabilization guidance
- queue clarity

Do not use notes to compare coordinators, rank people, report productivity, or expose behavior externally.
