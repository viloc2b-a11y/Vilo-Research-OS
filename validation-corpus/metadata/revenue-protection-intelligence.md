# Revenue Protection Intelligence (ClinIQ Integration)

## 1. Overview
Clinical compliance is not just about science; it is about site survival. If a site executes a protocol poorly, the Sponsor will not pay them. VIP translates clinical risk into Financial Risk, intended for integration with the ClinIQ financial suite.

## 2. Protection Vectors

### The "Open Query" Payment Hold
- **What the Sponsor does:** Withholds $1,500 Visit 3 payment because 2 queries remain unresolved for >30 days.
- **VIP Defense:** "2 Queries aging past 25 days. Revenue at Risk: $1,500. Action: Alert Site Director of impending cash flow leakage. Prioritize CRC tasks by dollar-value at risk."

### The "Missed Endpoint" Withholding
- **What the Sponsor does:** Refuses to pay the $5,000 Milestone for Visit 6 because the primary MRI scan was not performed according to protocol.
- **VIP Defense:** "Critical Procedure omitted. Probability of Payment Denial: 100%. Revenue at Risk: $5,000. Action: Flag visit as financially compromised. Trigger negotiation task with Sponsor for partial payment."

### The "SDV Backlog" Cash Flow Choke
- **What the Sponsor does:** CTAs often state "Payments rendered 30 days after CRA SDV completion."
- **VIP Defense:** "30 visits completed but unmonitored. Revenue Locked: $45,000. Action: Generate report for Site Director to demand a CRA monitoring visit from the Sponsor to unlock cash flow."

## 3. Site Impact
By converting abstract compliance metrics (Deviations, Queries) into hard currency ($), VIP forces site management to care about data quality. It aligns the financial incentives of the site business with the scientific needs of the trial.
