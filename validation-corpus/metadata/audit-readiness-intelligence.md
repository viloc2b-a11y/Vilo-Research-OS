# Audit Readiness Intelligence

## 1. Overview
An FDA BIMO (Bioresearch Monitoring) inspection can occur unannounced or with minimal warning. "Audit Readiness" means the site is perpetually in a state of absolute compliance.

## 2. Readiness Vectors

### The "Missing ICF" Audit Failure
- **What the FDA will see:** Missing or improperly signed Informed Consent Form (ICF). (The #1 FDA citation).
- **VIP Defense:** "Continuous Audit Sweep detects Subject 005 has procedures logged but no scanned ICF. Readiness Score: CRITICAL FAIL. Action: Administrative lock on Subject 005 chart until ICF is uploaded and verified."

### The "Open CAPA" Audit Failure
- **What the FDA will see:** A CAPA was opened 6 months ago for missing PK draws, but the "Preventive Action" (staff retraining) was never documented.
- **VIP Defense:** "CAPA #102 deadline approaches in 3 days. Readiness Score: DECLINING. Action: Escalate to Quality Director. Block PI from signing new trial contracts until legacy CAPA is closed."

### The "Regulatory Binder" Audit Failure
- **What the FDA will see:** IRB approval letter for Protocol Amendment 2 is missing from the binder.
- **VIP Defense:** "Amendment 2 active in system, but IRB approval document not linked. Readiness Score: AT RISK. Action: Alert Regulatory Coordinator."

## 3. Site Impact
Audit Readiness is the ultimate peace of mind for the Principal Investigator. VIP acts as an automated, relentless internal auditor that never sleeps, ensuring the site survives federal scrutiny.
