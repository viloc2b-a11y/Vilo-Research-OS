Protocol Number: PROTOCOL_A001
Protocol Title: A Phase 2 Study of Investigational Product in Adrenal Insufficiency
Brief Title: PROTOCOL_A001 adrenal study
Sponsor: SPONSOR_A
CRO: CRO_A
Phase 2
Indication: Adrenal insufficiency
Investigational Product: INVESTIGATIONAL_PRODUCT_A tablets
Study Design: Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multi-center
Enrollment target: 120
Study Duration: approximately 24 weeks

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Adults aged 18-75 years with documented adrenal insufficiency.
2. Able to provide informed consent and comply with visit schedule.
3. Stable concomitant medications for at least 30 days prior to Day 1.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Uncontrolled cardiovascular disease.
2. Pregnancy or breastfeeding.
3. Prior exposure to INVESTIGATIONAL_PRODUCT_A within 12 months.

Schedule of Events:
Visit 1: Screening (window -7 / 0) — site visit
Visit 2: Day 1 / Randomization (window -1 / +3) — site visit
Visit 3: Day 15 Treatment (window -3 / +3) — site visit
Visit 4: Day 39 On-Treatment / Cortisol window (window -3 / +3) — site visit
Visit 5: Day 56 Follow-up phone (window -3 / +3) — phone visit
Visit 6: Day 84 On-Treatment (window -7 / +7) — site visit
Visit 7: Arm A phone monitoring Day 30 (window -2 / +2) — phone visit Arm A
Visit 8: Arm B remote monitoring Day 30 (window -2 / +2) — remote visit Arm B
Visit 9: End of Study (window -14 / +14) — site visit

Procedure: Informed Consent (required)
